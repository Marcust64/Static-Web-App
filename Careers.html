Pilot Jobs

Why not start your flying career at Fly Ways? First Officer Pilot requirements area to have at least a commercial pilot certificate multi-engine land, 1500 hours total time, 1000 hours PIC, and a 1st class medical. We will put you through a training enviorment to give you a Type Certification in the Challenger or Global aircraft. After flying for at least 1000 hour as captain with us you will be able to flow into Delta Airlines as a first officer.
