About Us

Background

Jet Way was created in 2018 by John Jenkins and his co-founder Matt Roberts. We have location spread out thorugh the east coast at major airports such as GSO, RIC, PHL, MYR, ATL, and MCO to name a few. We continue to grow with our new partnership with Delta Airlines by expanding our locations and fleet size.

Fleet

Our Fleet consists of 30 Bombardier Global passenger jets and 10 Bombardier Challenger jets. We recently placed an order for 10 additional Challenger jets which we will be expecting to add in 2027. Challengers can seat up to 14 passengers and up to 12 suitcases, and the Global seats up to 16 passengers and 14 suitcases.
